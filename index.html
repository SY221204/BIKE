01 Harley-Davidson Fat Boy 114: A Legend on Two Wheels

The Harley-Davidson Fat Boy 114 is more than just a motorcycle—it’s an icon of power, style, and attitude. With its unmistakable muscular stance, solid-disc Lakester wheels, and Milwaukee-Eight 114 engine, the Fat Boy 114 commands attention wherever it goes..

A Bold and Timeless Design

First introduced in 1990, the Fat Boy quickly became one of Harley-Davidson’s most recognizable models, thanks to its solid wheels, wide front end, and imposing presence. Over the years, it has evolved while staying true to its roots. The latest Fat Boy 114 continues this legacy with modern refinements, featuring:
  • Satin chrome finishes that give it a bold yet refined look.
  • 240mm rear and 160mm front tires, the widest factory tires Harley-Davidson has ever put on a Softail.
  • Signature LED lighting, ensuring visibility and a distinctive appearance.

Performance & Power: Milwaukee-Eight 114

At the heart of the Fat Boy 114 is the Milwaukee-Eight 114 V-Twin engine, delivering 94 horsepower and 119 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse ensures thrilling acceleration and an unmistakable Harley-Davidson rumble that resonates with every twist of the throttle.
Key performance highlights:
  • 1,868cc displacement for a torque-rich riding experience.
  • Refined Softail chassis that makes it lighter and more agile than previous generations.
  • Comfortable yet aggressive riding position, perfect for both long highway rides and city cruising

Why the Fat Boy 114 Stands Out

  • A True Cruiser – With its low seat height (26.6 inches) and relaxed ergonomics, the Fat Boy is designed for rider comfort and long-haul enjoyment.
  • Heritage & Legacy – Famously ridden by Arnold Schwarzenegger in Terminator 2: Judgment Day, the Fat Boy carries a pop-culture legacy like no other.
  • Customization Potential – Whether you want a blacked-out beast or a chrome-drenched masterpiece, the Fat Boy 114 offers endless customization options.
